    High availability:

        High availability prevents system outages by building fault tpolarece and redundancy into the infrastructure

        1.Multi availability zone deployment:
            You should deploy your resources [EC2 , databases] accross at least two different availability zones.
            If one AZ experience a power outage or ntural disaster, the other remains online

        2.Elastic Load Balancing (ELB):
            Works  hand in hand with Autoscalling feature to automatically distribute the incoming traffic accross  multiple EC2 instances.
            ,routing request to only our healthy target.

        3.Multi-Region deployment:
            For maximum availability,application can be deploy globally accross multiple AWS regions [Using services like Amazon Route S3 for DNS routing], allowing for 
            disaster recovery and extency
