The CAP theorm 

The CAP Theorm is a key idea in distributed systems that explains why building a perfect reliable , networked data system is imposible.

Proposed by Eric Brwer and proven by Seth Gilbert and along with nancy lynch.
It states that a distributed system cannot guarantee Consistency , Avaialability,and Partition tolerance all at the same tine.
, and it can fully achieve any two (maximum) at a time
